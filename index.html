Welcome to Vietnamese Heart to Heart Baptist Church

Gather With Us Sundays, In-Person & Online. Sunday gatherings are the primary way we gather as a church to worship God together, learn the Scriptures, pray for one and others, and create space to hear from the Holy Spirit. We also gather in similar age groups and stages of life to grow our Faith together.
